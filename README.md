#  Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on E-commerce Sales Dataset

##  Project Overview

This project was completed as part of my **Data Analyst Internship at DecodeLabs**. The objective was to perform **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on an e-commerce sales dataset using **Microsoft Excel** to understand customer purchasing behavior, identify trends, discover hidden patterns, detect outliers, and generate meaningful business insights for decision-making.

The dataset consists of **1,200 customer orders** with information such as product details, quantity, unit price, payment methods, coupon usage, referral sources, order status, and order dates.

# 🎯 Objectives

- Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Assess data quality
- Calculate descriptive statistics
- Analyze product performance
- Identify customer purchasing behavior
- Discover trends and hidden patterns
- Detect outliers
- Generate business insights and recommendations
- Present findings using charts and Pivot Tables

# 🔍 Hidden Patterns Identified

The analysis revealed several hidden patterns, including:

- Product demand is balanced across multiple categories.
- Customers use all payment methods with only slight differences.
- Instagram is the leading referral source.
- Free shipping promotions are used more frequently than other coupons.
- Monthly revenue fluctuates throughout the year.
- A small number of high-value orders increase the overall average order value.

---

# 📈 Trends Identified

Time-based analysis revealed:

- Monthly sales variation
- Highest revenue month
- Yearly revenue comparison
- Seasonal fluctuations in sales performance

---

# ⚠️ Outlier Analysis

The Total Price column contains several unusually high-value transactions compared to the majority of customer orders.

These transactions may represent:

- Bulk purchases
- Premium product purchases
- High-value customer orders

Rather than removing them, they were considered valuable business information.

---

# 💡 Business Insights

The analysis provided several business insights:

- Customers purchase approximately three products per order.
- Chair generated the highest revenue.
- Chair was also the most purchased product.
- Online payment is the most commonly used payment method.
- FREESHIP is the most frequently used coupon.
- Instagram is the strongest customer acquisition channel.
- June recorded the highest monthly sales.
- Product demand is evenly distributed across categories.
- Average order value is influenced by a small number of expensive transactions.
